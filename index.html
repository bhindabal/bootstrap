Who We Are

BDS Trucking opened it's doors in February, 2001, however we have over 17 years of combined experience to insure optimal performance and professionalism. We are Minority Certified, DOT Certified and maintain a Disadvantaged Business Enterprise status in the state of California. Our services include, but are not limited to, hauling and dumping. We will also remove or bring in material to your construction site. Dump fees are included in most of our prices, and to receive a quote simply give us a call. We also have many opportunities for Driving Employment, Just visit our Careers Section for details. Our Office Hours are 8:30- 4:30 M-F, with Saturday scheduling available on request.
